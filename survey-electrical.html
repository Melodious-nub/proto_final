Electrical Safety Checklist


Operation and Maintenance:


1. Thermographic scanning inspection of electrical equipment done and results kept on site:
2. Factory must have as-built electrical single line diagram for proper maintenance of their electrical system:
3. Insulation resistance test of electrical equipment done and results kept on site:
4. Training on electrical safety program conducted:

Generator:


1. Adequate size of the generator room available in order to properly access the generator to perform routine maintenance activity:
2. Presence of combustible material in the generator room:
3. Properly rated generator room and also physically separated from the remainder of the building:
4. Earthing of the frame of the generator done at two different points:

Substation:


1. A transformer oil analysis is routinely completed on main service transformers:
2. The substation room has the required fire rating/protection and is physically separated from the remainder of the building:
3. Electrical insulation mats provided in front of substation:
4. Dust and lint not present on the transformer body:
5. All internal components of switchboards and distribution boards properly concealed:

Wiring and Cable:


1. Gap around cables passing through wall is sealed:
2. Wires not exposed while extending concealed wiring point:
3. Dust and lint not present in cable duct:
4. Cable trenches are properly supported:
5. Wire splicing is avoided:
